Frankincense Trading: From Resin to Essential Oil – A Legacy of Purity

Frankincense has been treasured for centuries for its aromatic and medicinal properties, and our company continues this legacy by providing the highest quality frankincense products available on the market today. We offer a wide range of frankincense forms to cater to the varying needs of our clients, from pure resin to essential oil.

Diverse Product Range

We understand that our clients have different preferences and requirements, which is why we provide frankincense in various forms:


Manufactured or Pure Resin: Ideal for traditional uses, including incense burning and rituals.
Essential Oil: Perfect for aromatherapy and natural skincare products, our essential oil is derived from the finest resin.
Frankincense Water: Used for its calming properties, it’s a popular choice for health and wellness applications.
Residue Powder: This by-product of resin production can be used in various industrial applications.
Original Product: For those who value authenticity, we offer raw, unprocessed frankincense in its natural form.

Commitment to Quality and Sustainability

Our frankincense is sourced from regions known for their premium quality resin. We adhere to strict harvesting and processing standards to ensure that the final product is of the highest quality. Furthermore, we are committed to sustainable practices, ensuring that the natural environment is preserved for future generations.

Global Distribution

With a strong logistics network, we distribute our frankincense products to clients around the world. Whether you are in the Middle East, Asia, or beyond, we ensure timely and efficient delivery, maintaining the integrity of the product throughout the supply chain.
