About Us

At HAKIMI TRADING COMPANY

We are committed to delivering excellence in the export of fish, frankincense, and livestock. With the capacity to supply more than 100 tons of our products each month, we cater to a global market, ensuring quality and reliability in every shipment.

Our fish exports include a wide range of frozen varieties, such as lobster and crayfish, available in any size and packaged according to client specifications. We take pride in our ability to customize our offerings, ensuring that our clients receive exactly what they need.

In addition to fish, we are a trusted supplier of frankincense in various forms: resin, oil, powder, and water. Whether for traditional uses or modern applications, our frankincense products are of the highest quality, meeting the demands of customers worldwide.

We also specialize in the export of livestock, including goats, sheep, and camels. Our livestock is available to clients around the globe, with a particular focus on providing for events such as the Islamic Hajj season, where the demand for quality livestock is at its peak.

Our company was founded on the belief that successful business is driven by effective communication and mutual benefit. Unfortunately, in our society, these elements are often lacking due to language barriers and a lack of business orientation. This has led to a dependence on middlemen, who are often non-professional and unreliable partners. Recognizing these challenges, we have dedicated ourselves to creating a direct, transparent, and professional business model.

In the process, we have also addressed one of the major obstacles in this industry: freight charges and transportation issues. After careful planning and persistent effort, we have successfully resolved these challenges, ensuring that our products reach our clients efficiently and cost-effectively.

At HAKIMI TRADING COMPANY, we are not just exporters; we are problem-solvers, dedicated to bridging gaps and creating opportunities for our clients and partners worldwide.
